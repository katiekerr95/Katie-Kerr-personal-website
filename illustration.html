Illustration

I started drawing in an empty sketchbook when I came to college, and never really stopped. My preferred materials are Sharpies, pens, colored pencils, pastels, and watercolor pencils, but I dabble in other media as well. The illustration pieces below mostly fall into a few categories: radials, where I start in the middle with a small shape and draw outwards, adding layers around as I go; abstract, where I pull inspiration from the natural world - under the sea, in particular; typographic illustrations, where I collect quotes and sayings that have an impact on me, and make typographic choices to best illustrate the tone and mood of the quote.
