Custom Longboard Design

During the summer of 2014, I ordered a blank longboard online and taught myself to board. While learning, I began to plan my own design to go on the bottom of the board. The final design was actually completed as I drove from Denver to Boston to move into my current apartment for school.

I love the duality of the sayings "c'est la vie" (meaning 'that is life' in French), and "shit happens," a cruder version with the same intent. The flowy, organic design of "c'est la vie" contrasts with the frenetic, geometric pattern of "shit happens." The positive black letters of "c'est la vie" eventually breaks down into the barbed, interwoven pieces, whose negative space reads "shit happens." They are opposite versions of the same meaning, yin and yang responses of acceptance to the absurdity of life.
