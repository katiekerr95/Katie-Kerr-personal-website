About

I graduated from Northeastern University in May 2018 with a BFA in Experience Design. Additionally, I am active within the Maker community, and have extensive experience with digital fabrication, especially lasercutting.

In the last few years, I have created many public art pieces, including murals, an interactive coloring poster, and public art installations. My most recent piece is a 20-foot acrylic mural, commissioned from Northeastern University.

I also produced a coloring book in Fall 2016, and currently sell stickers and prints of my illustration and hand-lettering.

Besides art and design, my hobbies include hiking and backpacking, cooking, longboarding, and striking up conversations with friendly strangers.
